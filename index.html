Features

The Fender Stratocaster, introduced in 1954, is renowned for its sleek double-cutaway body, comfortable contours, and versatile tonal capabilities. Featuring three single-coil pickups and a synchronized tremolo system, it offers a wide range of tones from bright and twangy to warm and smooth. With its five-way pickup selector switch, players can easily craft their desired sound. Loved by musicians across genres, the Stratocaster remains an enduring symbol of innovation and musical excellence.
